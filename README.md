**#Superstore_Sample_Report**
Transformed raw transactional data into actionable insights to identify **high-profit product categories** and **regional sales trends**.
Identified **Technology** as the highest profit-generating category while pinpointing regional dips, specifically in the **Central region** to help target operational improvements.
Integrated **multi-city filters** and visual guages to provide stakeholders with a quick, drill-down view of sales volume (38k units) and profit margins per segment.
